Hello World, it's Chang here!

I am a 2nd year PhD student at the University of Utah in the School of Computing and SCI Institute. I got my B.Eng. degree from Shandong University.

My current research is mainly in the field of Human Computer Interaction and Data Visualization. I care about using visualization as a communication medium to convey knowledge and insights from data. I also find joy in crafting niche visualization designs and engineering automations that animate these designs.

Outside of research, I watch and play table tennis and was part of the school team. I also enjoy outdoor activities (e.g., hiking & camping). When I am on vacation, I spend time playing video games apart from having fun with friends.

Feel free to drop me an email!
