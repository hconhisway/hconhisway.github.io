Hello World, it's Chang here!

I am now a research assistant at Interdisciplinary Reasearch Center (IRC) of Shandong University, and currently a member of IDEAS Lab, working with Prof. Yunhai Wang. I got my B.Eng. degree from the same Univ. in Fall 2022 after a joyful four-year journey.

My current exploration of research are mostly on the field of Data Visualization, focusing especially on the visualization of graphs & trees. Despite having broad interest in VIS | HCI | AI, the very beginning mind of me doing research is to help people understand the enormous data around them and make good use of this information. For me, these techniques are great because they can bridge the visible phisical world and the invisible Cyberspace where most data is contained.

Outside of research, I watch and play table tennis and was part of the school team. I also enjoy outdoor activities (e.g., hiking & camping). When I am on vacation, I spend time playing video games (alone) apart from having fun with friends.

This is an incomplete site with tons of content under preparation. Please feel free to drop me an email if you would like to learn more (or just want to chat)!
